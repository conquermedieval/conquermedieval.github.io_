Archcraft XFCE

This flavor of Archcraft comes with XFCE. Xfce is a lightweight desktop environment, It aims to be fast and low on system resources. Archcraft XFCE is highly customized and pre-configured. It's a ready to go XFCE system.

Archcraft Lite

Archcraft Lite is another flavor of Archcraft, targeted at intermediate/power Linux users. Unlike main Archcraft, It does not provide you anything fancy. Just a single window manager with few basic applications and utilities.
